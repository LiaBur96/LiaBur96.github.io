ABOUT US

Our Story:

GiVaeKo is a brand started by Michael Thomas,
the grandfather aka "Paw Paw" of the group.
He started this brand up because he wanted his
three grandchildren to have something handed
down to them when they got older. This allows
for all of our family to be involved in many
different ways. Check out our team to get a
better understanding of how we all get involved.

Meet The Team:

Michael Thomas: Founder of GiVaeKo and the start of our
generation. He started this brand from the bottom up by
purchasing all of the material and setting up social
media pages to get the brand name out to the public.
He creates all of the images from scratch or designs
whatever the customer may want.

Melissa Thomas: Grandma of GiVaeko and the quality
inspector of any material being shipped or delivered.
She ensures all designs meet the customers requests
by inspecting each individual item.

Hydia Thomas: Daughter of GiVaeKo and social media
administrator. She ensures new items are posted to
social media and goes live to answer any questions
customers may have. She will sometimes make designs
while on live to show customers the quality and
time put into our designs.

Melia Burrell: Daughter of GiVaeko and website
administrator. Any updated items that need to be
added or removed, she takes care of it. She also
assists with creating designs if customers need
a little assistance expressing their requests.

Ben and David: The muscle of GiVaeKo. They assist
with heavy/bulk orders that require extra strength
for shipping and handling.

Gianna, Nevaeh, and Niko: The grandchildren of
the group and the reason "Paw Paw" created it.
They just assist us with modeling any gear and
apparel that's created for them.
